## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does a typical project take?
A: Project timelines vary based on complexity:
- Simple automations: 1-3 weeks
- Medium complexity projects: 4-8 weeks
- Complex system integrations: 8-16 weeks
We provide specific timelines in our project proposals.

### Q: What industries do you typically work with?
A: We work with a wide range of industries including professional services, retail, healthcare, manufacturing, and finance. Our solutions are customized to meet industry-specific requirements and regulations.

### Q: How do you ensure data security and privacy?
A: We follow strict security protocols including encrypted data transmission, secure storage, limited access controls, and compliance with relevant regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.). Security measures are detailed in our contracts.

### Q: What happens after the project is completed?
A: We provide post-completion support during a transition period. We also offer ongoing support packages for continued optimization and maintenance. Many clients engage us for additional projects as their business evolves.

### Q: Can you work with our existing IT team?
A: Absolutely! We often collaborate with existing IT teams to ensure seamless integration and knowledge transfer. We adapt to your organization's existing processes and procedures.
