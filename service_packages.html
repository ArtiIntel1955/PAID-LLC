Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service packages

What's included in the AI Assessment Package?

The AI Assessment Package includes a comprehensive evaluation of your organization's current state, identification of AI opportunities, selection of appropriate tools, and development of a strategic roadmap. You'll receive a detailed written report with ROI projections, a 2-hour strategy session with our experts, and access to our proprietary assessment tools and templates.

How long does the Implementation Package take?

The timeline for the Implementation Package varies based on the complexity and scope of your project, but typically ranges from 4-12 weeks. The process includes assessment review, tool setup and configuration, workflow automation, team training, and go-live support. We provide regular updates and milestones throughout the implementation process.

Can I upgrade from Assessment to Implementation?

Absolutely! Many of our clients start with the AI Assessment Package to understand their opportunities and then upgrade to the Implementation Package. When you upgrade, we apply the assessment fee toward your implementation cost, making the transition seamless and cost-effective.

What happens during the 30-day post-launch support?

During the 30-day post-launch support period, we monitor your AI solution's performance, address any issues that arise, provide additional training as needed, and make adjustments to optimize results. This ensures a smooth transition and maximizes the success of your AI implementation.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes, we offer custom packages tailored to specific business needs. If our standard packages don't fully meet your requirements, we can create a customized solution that addresses your unique challenges and objectives. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and receive a personalized quote.
