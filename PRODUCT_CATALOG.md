## Delivery System Architecture

### Automated Delivery Process
1. **Purchase Confirmation**: Customer completes purchase
2. **Email Verification**: System verifies customer email
3. **Product Assembly**: System compiles all relevant files
4. **Secure Link Generation**: Creates time-limited download link
5. **Email Delivery**: Sends download link and instructions
6. **Access Logging**: Tracks download attempts
7. **Follow-up**: Sends satisfaction survey after 7 days
