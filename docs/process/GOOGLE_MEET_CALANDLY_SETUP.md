# Google Meet + Calendly Integration Setup Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how to integrate Google Meet with your Calendly account to provide seamless video conferencing for client meetings.

## Prerequisites
- Active Calendly account using ConnectwithPAID@outlook.com
- Google account (can be linked to your ConnectwithPAID@outlook.com)
- Calendly paid plan (Basic plan or higher required for video conferencing integration)

## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Prepare Your Google Account
1. Go to https://calendar.google.com
2. Sign in with a Google account (you can link this to your ConnectwithPAID@outlook.com)
3. If using Outlook/Exchange, you may need to add your Outlook calendar to Google Calendar:
   - Go to Settings > Import & Export
   - Add your Outlook calendar via the subscription option

### Step 2: Connect Google Calendar to Calendly
1. Log in to your Calendly account
2. Go to "Account" > "Calendar Connections"
3. Click "Connect" next to Google Calendar
4. Follow the authentication prompts to connect your Google account
5. Select the calendar you want to use for meetings

### Step 3: Set Up Google Meet
1. In Google Calendar, create a test event to verify Meet is working
2. Ensure that Google Meet is enabled in your Google Calendar settings:
   - Go to Google Calendar settings
   - Under "Video Conferencing," select "Google Meet"

### Step 4: Configure Calendly Event Types for Video Meetings
1. In Calendly, go to "Event Types"
2. Edit an existing event type or create a new one
3. Under "Location," select "Online Meeting" 
4. Choose "Google Meet" as the conferencing option
5. Customize the meeting settings:
   - Meeting agenda (optional)
   - Meeting description
   - Recording permissions
   - Waiting room settings

### Step 5: Configure Advanced Settings
1. In your event type settings:
   - Set buffer times before/after meetings
   - Configure cancellation policies
   - Set availability rules
   - Configure notification settings
   - Add any custom questions for client intake

### Step 6: Test the Integration
1. Copy your Calendly event link
2. Book a test appointment using a different email
3. Verify that:
   - Calendar invitation is sent correctly
   - Google Meet link appears in the calendar entry
   - Meeting joins successfully
   - Notifications are received as expected

## Troubleshooting Tips

### Common Issues:
1. **Google Meet not appearing in Calendly**: Ensure you have a paid Calendly plan
2. **Calendar sync delays**: Allow up to 15 minutes for calendar synchronization
3. **Meeting link not working**: Check that Google Meet is properly enabled in your Google Calendar settings

### Verification Steps:
1. Check that your Google Calendar shows the meeting when booked via Calendly
2. Confirm that attendees receive the Google Meet link in their confirmation email
3. Test joining the meeting from both organizer and attendee perspectives

## Security Considerations
- Enable waiting room feature to control who joins meetings
- Set up meeting passwords for additional security
- Disable "Join before host" if you prefer to enter meetings first
- Review privacy settings for recording permissions

## Best Practices
- Test the entire booking and meeting process before going live
- Set appropriate buffer times between meetings
- Customize meeting descriptions to reflect your brand
- Monitor calendar availability to prevent double-bookings
- Regularly review meeting statistics in Calendly
