# AI Implementation Preparation Checklist

## Purpose
Ensure your organization is fully prepared for successful AI implementation with this comprehensive checklist covering all critical preparation areas.

---

## PHASE 1: ASSESSMENT & PLANNING

### Leadership Alignment
- [ ] Leadership team understands AI potential and limitations
- [ ] Clear AI vision and goals established
- [ ] AI initiative assigned to a dedicated project champion
- [ ] Executive sponsor identified and committed
- [ ] Success metrics defined and agreed upon
- [ ] Risk tolerance and mitigation strategies determined

### Budget Planning
- [ ] AI implementation budget approved
- [ ] Ongoing operational costs planned for
- [ ] Training and change management budget allocated
- [ ] Contingency funds reserved for unexpected costs
- [ ] ROI expectations documented and realistic

### Needs Analysis
- [ ] Specific business challenges identified for AI solutions
- [ ] Current processes mapped and documented
- [ ] Desired future state defined
- [ ] Gap analysis completed between current and desired states
- [ ] Quick wins vs. long-term initiatives prioritized

---

## PHASE 2: TECHNICAL PREPARATION

### Data Assessment
- [ ] Data inventory completed
- [ ] Data quality evaluated and improved where needed
- [ ] Data governance policies established
- [ ] Data privacy and security measures confirmed
- [ ] Data accessibility and integration requirements identified
- [ ] Backup and recovery procedures validated

### Infrastructure Evaluation
- [ ] Current IT infrastructure assessed for AI compatibility
- [ ] Internet connectivity verified as reliable and sufficient
- [ ] Security protocols reviewed and updated as needed
- [ ] Integration requirements with existing systems documented
- [ ] Scalability considerations addressed
- [ ] IT support resources allocated for AI initiatives

### Platform Selection
- [ ] AI tools evaluated against business requirements
- [ ] Vendor due diligence completed
- [ ] Licensing and subscription models understood
- [ ] Integration capabilities confirmed
- [ ] Security and compliance requirements met
- [ ] Total cost of ownership calculated

---

## PHASE 3: PEOPLE & PROCESS PREPARATION

### Skills Assessment
- [ ] Current team AI skills inventory completed
- [ ] Training needs identified and planned
- [ ] Change management strategy developed
- [ ] Internal AI champions identified and trained
- [ ] Support resources for users established
- [ ] Performance measurement criteria defined

### Process Redesign
- [ ] Existing workflows analyzed for AI integration
- [ ] New AI-enhanced processes designed
- [ ] Process documentation updated
- [ ] Quality assurance procedures adapted for AI outputs
- [ ] Approval and validation workflows defined
- [ ] Error handling and escalation procedures established

### Communication Plan
- [ ] Internal communication strategy for AI rollout
- [ ] Stakeholder expectations managed
- [ ] Training schedule communicated
- [ ] Feedback mechanisms established
- [ ] Success story sharing plan created
- [ ] Resistance management strategies prepared
