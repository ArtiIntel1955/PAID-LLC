## RESULTS INTERPRETATION

**Calculate your score:**
- Add up all your selected ratings (possible range: 20-100 points)

**Scoring Guide:**
- **80-100 Points**: Excellent Readiness - You're well-positioned for AI implementation
- **60-79 Points**: Good Readiness - You have a solid foundation with some areas for improvement
- **40-59 Points**: Moderate Readiness - Several areas need strengthening before AI implementation
- **20-39 Points**: Limited Readiness - Significant preparation needed before AI implementation

---

## RECOMMENDATIONS BY SCORE RANGE

### 80-100 Points: Excellent Readiness
- Begin with pilot AI projects in low-risk areas
- Consider advanced AI solutions like Microsoft Copilot or Google Gemini
- Focus on measuring and optimizing ROI from AI investments

### 60-79 Points: Good Readiness
- Address the lowest-scoring areas before full implementation
- Start with simpler AI tools like ChatGPT for content creation
- Develop a phased implementation plan

### 40-59 Points: Moderate Readiness
- Focus on foundational improvements before AI adoption
- Invest in staff training and skill development
- Improve data organization and infrastructure

### 20-39 Points: Limited Readiness
- Prioritize organizational readiness and infrastructure
- Develop clear AI strategy and governance framework
- Consider working with an AI consultant to guide preparation
